What do I think about the future of the web!

Every day the web becomes stronger, faster and smarter. Software engineers from all over the world improve and develop the World Wide Web. Those people dedicate their lives to solve the problems and make every day’s experience of users easier and comfortable. The exploration of the web is an important evidence of the advancement of humanity. It means that in the future people will do everything by using their smart devices. I think that in 5 years the look of the web will be extremely changed. People will not need to open their computers for getting access to the Internet. The Wifi will be everywhere, free and fast. So, people will have an access to the Internet twenty-four hours and seven days in a week from any place on Earth. One of the necessary devices will be a special small thin box that will look like a rectangle with smooth angles. This device will fit any pocket and its weight will be less than a pen. By pressing on the touch screen on the surface of this device people easily will get the browser window in the air - hologram, that will allow them to see the interface of the welcome page where users can search everything they need by using their voices or fingers. White background, black navigation bar with light font will be the main welcome page that allows to search and browse. Also, there will be a moving colorful line with low opacity on the bottom. This line denotes an assistant like “Siri” or “Alexa” but with more knowledge and improved recognition voice function. Perhaps, Mark Zuckerberg and his company “Facebook” will be the providers of the “Jarvis” AI who will be a great partner for every single web user. The design is important for the future web because the users’ satisfaction and happiness of using it are in the priority. Therefore, the design of the web page will be very simple and a user-interface will flow freely, allowing people to navigate easily. The efficiency is very important, so, any problems will be solved by AI assistant in a few seconds. The mobile devices may disappear at all. Instead of it every person will have a chip in their hands that, as the small rectangle with smooth angels, will have hologram. The hologram will show the home page of the “cell phone” with all necessary icons and buttons. The design will be also simple and effective for users. The best web designers will provide every single device with the packet of personal fonts, new features, small tutorials, etc. for users. One of the strength sides of it is a fast connection with the Internet and using AI for getting more information and help. The web is one of the most powerful discoveries in the world. Every day people create new websites, apps, programs, etc. They write code and make people’s lives better. Without good design and creative people the world would be boring, not effective and behind the times. Design can make everything possible.
